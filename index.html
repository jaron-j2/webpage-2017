WHAT I DO

A simple summary of what I have to offer!
Click the expand button to see more!

MODERN DESIGN

Being someone who has self-diagnosed mild, situational, subjective OCD (obviously not legitimate), design, or rather the lack thereof it, constantly gets on my nerves for not being "perfect". Since most people's designs aren't up to standard, why not be my own designer and produce the product and styles I want? Playing around with Adobe Photoshop and Premiere plus a large dose of 'Material' and 'Minimalist' design awareness form the foundations of my design skills.

QUANTITATIVE REASONING

Two's company, three's a crowd - quantifying and reasoning can be ever so basic. Put those two terms together, however, and you require an expert. A background in Engineering has not merely leveraged on my abilities in the hard sciences and in arithmetics, it has refined the approach I take to breaking down big-scale problems. 70% of any quantitative research is in data collection and only 30% is in analysis. Marketing is no different; choose someone who can reason quantitatively.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Through sheer perseverance, diligence and focus had I attained a good grasp at web-development. Currently familiar with HTML5, jQuery and attempting to learn PHP. Started off as an interest and the thought that it is a "good skill to have", I am refining my development skills and learning new syntax everyday. If I accomplished so much in such a short span, I am looking forward to what I can offer in the future. I may not be the best developer now, but one day I will be.

SKILLED COMMUNICATOR

Being a good listener in addition to being a good speaker is essential to being an excellent communicator. As someone who is highly perceptive during interactions and really street-smart, communicating views and ideas in professional settings is a task I have great confidence in. Always take a sincere interest in what others have to say, put in time to ask the right questions, listen well, and pick up on the little things.

TEAM MANAGEMENT

Years of undertaking responsibilities and leadership positions have chiselled me into a respectable leader and team member. Team management is not about being a glorified leader, it is a skill that requires tact and a strive to maximise your team's potential whether by being a leader or a collaborator. In a world of many great minds of varying disciplines, a team manager is needed in every team to bring everyone together.

SOCIAL WORK

Always willing to contribute back to society, social work has always been a passion of mine. Coming from an underprivileged household, I am where I am today thanks to the guidance and inspiration the figures of my past have provided. As much as possible, I am motivated to be that guiding figure for other disadvantaged youths. Apart from being a voluntary mentor and tutor, I often contribute my skills too such as in design and marketing.
