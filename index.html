MODERN DESIGN

Being someone who has self-diagnosed mild, situational, subjective OCD (obviously not legitimate), design, or rather the lack thereof it, constantly gets on my nerves for not being "perfect". Since most people's designs aren't up to standard, why not be my own designer and produce the product and styles I want? Playing around with Adobe Photoshop and Premiere plus a large dose of 'Material' and 'Minimalist' design awareness form the foundations of my design skills.

QUANTITATIVE REASONING

Two's company, three's a crowd - quantifying and reasoning can be ever so basic. Put those two terms together, however, and you require an expert. A background in Engineering has not merely leveraged on my abilities in the hard sciences and in arithmetics, it has refined the approach I take to breaking down big-scale problems. 70% of any quantitative research is in data collection and only 30% is in analysis. Marketing is no different; choose someone who can reason quantitatively.
